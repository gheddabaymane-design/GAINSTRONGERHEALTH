General Questions

How long does it take to see results with Trimology? +

Most customers notice improvements within 2-4 weeks of consistent use. Some notice changes sooner, while others may take up to 30 days. For best results, maintain consistent daily use and combine it with a healthy diet and regular physical activity. Individual results vary based on lifestyle, diet, metabolism, and starting point.

Is Trimology safe? Does it have side effects? +

Trimology is formulated with natural ingredients and is well-tolerated by most people. Our ingredients are third-party tested for purity and potency. Most users experience no side effects. However, some people may experience mild caffeine-related effects since we include a natural caffeine component. If you have specific health conditions, are pregnant or nursing, or take medications, please consult your healthcare provider before starting any supplement.

Can I take Trimology with other supplements or medications? +

Generally, yes, Trimology can be taken with other supplements. However, we strongly recommend consulting your healthcare provider or pharmacist first. They can review all your current supplements and medications to ensure there are no interactions. This is especially important if you're on prescription medications or other active supplements.

Dosage & Usage

How should I take Trimology? +

Take 2 capsules daily with a glass of water, preferably in the morning or as recommended on the package label. You can take it with or without food, though some people prefer taking it with breakfast. For best results, take it consistently every day at the same time.

Can I take more than the recommended dose? +

No, we don't recommend exceeding the recommended dosage. More is not better with supplements. The formula is precisely balanced for optimal results and safety. Taking more than recommended could lead to unnecessary side effects. Stick to 2 capsules daily for the best experience.

What if I forget to take a dose? +

If you forget a dose, simply take it when you remember, as long as it's not close to your next scheduled dose. Just skip the missed dose if it's almost time for your next one. Don't double up on doses. Consistency is important, so try to take it at the same time each day to build a habit.

Results & Lifestyle

Will Trimology work without diet and exercise? +

Trimology is designed to support your weight management efforts, not replace healthy lifestyle choices. While some users report positive changes even without major diet changes, the best results come from combining Trimology with a balanced diet and regular physical activity. Think of Trimology as a tool that makes your healthy habits more effective, not a substitute for them.

Can I drink coffee while taking Trimology? +

Yes, you can. Trimology contains a modest amount of natural caffeine (50mg per serving). If you're sensitive to caffeine, you may want to be mindful of your total caffeine intake from all sources including coffee, tea, and other beverages. A typical cup of coffee contains 95-200mg of caffeine, so your total daily intake would be moderate.

Are there any foods I should avoid while taking Trimology? +

No specific foods need to be avoided. However, for best results, we recommend maintaining a balanced diet rich in whole foods, lean proteins, vegetables, and healthy fats. Trimology is designed to support healthy choices, so the quality of your overall diet matters. Stay hydrated and avoid excessive processed foods and added sugars.

Storage & Shelf Life

How should I store Trimology? +

Store Trimology in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture. Keep the bottle tightly closed. Room temperature is ideal. Avoid storing in bathrooms or kitchens where temperature and humidity fluctuate. If stored properly, Trimology maintains its potency for the full shelf life listed on the bottle.

What's the shelf life of Trimology? +

The shelf life is clearly marked on each bottle. Typically, sealed bottles of Trimology remain stable for 24 months from the manufacturing date when stored properly. Always check the expiration date on your bottle before use. Once opened, use within the timeframe recommended on the label.
