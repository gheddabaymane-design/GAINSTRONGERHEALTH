Key Ingredients

Green Tea Extract (EGCG)
100mg per serving
Metabolism Support
Antioxidant

Green tea extract is rich in EGCG (epigallocatechin gallate), a powerful compound that supports thermogenesis — your body's natural calorie-burning process. Unlike caffeine alone, EGCG provides sustained metabolic support.

Research: Multiple studies published in the American Journal of Clinical Nutrition confirm EGCG's effectiveness in supporting healthy weight management.
Garcinia Cambogia (Hydroxycitric Acid)
500mg per serving
Appetite Control
Craving Reduction

Garcinia Cambogia contains hydroxycitric acid (HCA), which helps reduce appetite by increasing serotonin levels. This means fewer cravings and better satiety without relying on willpower alone.

Research: Studies show that HCA can help reduce caloric intake by supporting natural appetite regulation mechanisms.
Conjugated Linoleic Acid (CLA)
250mg per serving
Body Composition
Metabolism

CLA is a naturally occurring fatty acid that supports healthy metabolism and promotes the body's natural ability to maintain lean muscle mass while supporting weight management goals.

Research: Clinical trials demonstrate CLA's role in supporting healthy body composition through enhanced metabolic function.
Caffeine (Natural from Coffee Bean)
50mg per serving
Energy Support
Mental Clarity

A modest dose of natural caffeine to provide sustained mental clarity and energy without the jitters. Combined with adaptogens for smooth, all-day support.

Research: Natural caffeine enhances focus and supports metabolic function without the crash of synthetic stimulants.
Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera)
200mg per serving
Stress Support
Energy Adapter

This ancient adaptogenic herb helps your body manage stress naturally. Since stress often triggers cravings and weight gain, supporting healthy stress levels supports your weight management goals.

Research: Multiple studies confirm Ashwagandha's role in supporting healthy cortisol levels and reducing stress-related eating patterns.
B-Complex Vitamins (B6, B12, B9)
Complete daily value
Energy Production
Metabolism Support

Essential B vitamins are cofactors in energy production. They ensure your body efficiently converts food into fuel, not stored fat. Also supports healthy metabolism of macronutrients.

Research: B vitamins are essential for optimal metabolic function and energy production at the cellular level.
Chromium Picolinate
200mcg per serving
Blood Sugar Support
Craving Control

Chromium helps regulate blood sugar levels, which is crucial for reducing carb cravings and maintaining steady energy. When blood sugar is stable, you're less likely to overeat.

Research: Studies show chromium supplementation supports healthy glucose metabolism and reduces cravings for refined carbohydrates.
